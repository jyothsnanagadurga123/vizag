Welcome to Vizag – The City of Destiny

Visakhapatnam (Vizag) is a beautiful coastal city in Andhra Pradesh.
It is famous for its scenic beaches, hill views, ports, and rich culture.
Vizag is a major industrial, educational, and tourist hub.

HOME CITY

Vizag is known for its calm lifestyle,
friendly people, clean beaches,
and natural beauty.

City

Visakhapatnam is one of the largest cities in Andhra Pradesh,
known for its ports, industries, IT hubs,
and coastal charm.

Port City

Vizag Port is one of India’s major seaports,
playing a key role in trade, navy operations,
and economic growth.

Kailasagiri

Kailasagiri is a hilltop park offering panoramic views
of the Bay of Bengal and the city,
and is a major tourist attraction.

Education

Vizag has prestigious institutions like
Andhra University, GITAM University,
and various engineering & medical colleges.

Beaches

Famous beaches include RK Beach,
Rushikonda Beach, Yarada Beach,
and Bheemili Beach.

Address
